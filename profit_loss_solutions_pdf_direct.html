Profit & Loss — Solutions PDF

Cheat Sheet

Profit = SP − CP
Loss = CP − SP
Profit% = (Profit / CP) × 100
Loss% = (Loss / CP) × 100
SP = CP × (100 + P%) / 100
SP = CP × (100 − L%) / 100
CP = SP × 100 / (100 + P%)
CP = SP × 100 / (100 − L%)
Discount = MP − SP
Discount% = (Discount / MP) × 100
SP = MP × (100 − Discount%) / 100
Successive discounts x%, y%: Net discount = x + y − (xy / 100)
Equal gain/loss x% → net loss = x² / 100 %
CP of n items = SP of m items → Profit% = (n − m) / m × 100
False weight profit% = Error / (True − Error) × 100

Solved Questions

Q1. A trader buys goods for ₹800 and sells at ₹1000. Find profit%.
Correct answer: 25%
Explanation: Profit=200, CP=800. Profit%=200/800×100=25%
Q2. SP=₹570, Loss%=5%. Find CP.
Correct answer: ₹600
Explanation: CP=SP×100/(100-L)=570×100/95=600
Q3. CP=₹500, Gain%=20%. Find SP.
Correct answer: ₹600
Explanation: SP=500×120/100=600
Q4. An item is sold at 5/6th of its MP at a loss of 10%. What is discount% on MP?
Correct answer: 16.67%
Explanation: SP=5/6 MP. SP also=CP×0.9. So CP=5MP/6×1/0.9. MP is 20% above CP, discount = MP-SP=MP/6 → 16.67% of MP.
Q5. CP of 36 articles = SP of 30 articles. Find profit%.
Correct answer: 20%
Explanation: Profit%=(36-30)/30×100=20%
Q6. A man sells two items at ₹1800 each — one at 20% gain, one at 20% loss. Overall?
Correct answer: 4% loss
Explanation: Same % gain & loss → loss%=x²/100=400/100=4% loss
Q7. After 30% discount on MP=₹400, what is SP?
Correct answer: ₹280
Explanation: SP=400×70/100=280
Q8. Successive discounts of 20% and 10%. Find net discount%.
Correct answer: 28%
Explanation: Net=20+10-20×10/100=28%
Q9. SP=₹1040, Gain%=4%. Find CP.
Correct answer: ₹1000
Explanation: CP=1040×100/104=1000
Q10. A trader marks 25% above CP and allows 10% discount. Find gain%.
Correct answer: 12.5%
Explanation: SP=CP×1.25×0.9=1.125 CP. Gain%=12.5%
Q11. Bought 120 m at ₹125/m. Sold 40% at 15% gain, rest at x% gain. Overall 25% gain. Find x.
Correct answer: 31.25%
Explanation: Set up equation: 40%×115+60%×(100+x)=125. Solve to get x=31.25%
Q12. Price of tea drops 10%, sales rise 10%. Find net % change in revenue.
Correct answer: 1% loss
Explanation: Net%=−10+10+(−10×10)/100=−1% loss
Q13. Ratio CP:SP=20:21. Find profit%.
Correct answer: 5%
Explanation: Profit%=(21-20)/20×100=5%
Q14. 6 articles bought at 4 for ₹6, sold at 6 for ₹4. Find gain/loss%?
Correct answer: 125% gain
Explanation: CP per article=6/4=1.5. SP per article=4/6=0.667. Wait: re-read. Buy 4 for ₹6 means CP=6/4=1.5. Sell at rate of 4 for ₹6: SP=6/4=1.5. Equal so 0%? Check: actually 'sold at 4 for ₹6'? Classic TCS: buy 6 for ₹4 (CP=4/6), sell 4 for ₹6 (SP=6/4). Profit%=(SP-CP)/CP×100=(1.5-0.667)/0.667×100=125%
Q15. A sells to B at 10% profit. B sells to C at 10% loss. C pays ₹990. What did A pay?
Correct answer: ₹1000
Explanation: Let A's CP=x. B's SP=1.1x. C pays=1.1x×0.9=0.99x=990 → x=1000
Q16. Marked price ₹800, two successive discounts 5% each. Find SP.
Correct answer: ₹722
Explanation: SP=800×0.95×0.95=722
Q17. A trader uses false weight of 900g instead of 1kg. Profit%?
Correct answer: 11.11%
Explanation: Error=100g. Profit%=100/(1000-100)×100=100/900×100=11.11%
Q18. If Akash buys 20 pens at cost x and sells 16 pens at cost x. Find gain%.
Correct answer: 25%
Explanation: CP per pen=x/20. SP per pen=x/16. Gain%=(x/16-x/20)/(x/20)×100=25%
Q19. MP is 10% less than CP. CP=₹200. Sold at 5% loss on MP. Find SP.
Correct answer: ₹171
Explanation: MP=200×0.9=180. SP=180×0.95=171
Q20. 400 handbags: 100 at 20% profit, rest at 10% profit. Find overall profit%.
Correct answer: 12.5%
Explanation: Overall=(100×120+300×110)/400=12.5% gain
Q21. Sold at ₹950, loss 5%. What price for 4% gain?
Correct answer: ₹1040
Explanation: CP=950×100/95=1000. SP for 4% gain=1040
Q22. Divyam buys 30 pens at cost x, sells 40 pens at cost x. Find loss%.
Correct answer: 25%
Explanation: CP/pen=x/30. SP/pen=x/40. Loss%=(x/30-x/40)/(x/30)×100=25%
Q23. Electronic dealer earns 5% profit even after 30% discount. If he offers 3 successive 12% discounts on MP, approximate min profit%?
Correct answer: Approx -1%
Explanation: SP after 30% disc=0.7MP=1.05CP so MP=1.5CP. After 3×12% disc: SP=MP×0.88³=MP×0.681=0.681×1.5CP≈1.022CP ≈2% profit.
Q24. CP of 5 apples = SP of 4 apples. Find profit%.
Correct answer: 25%
Explanation: Profit%=(5-4)/4×100=25%
Q25. A sells at 12% profit to B who sells at 8% profit to C. C pays ₹756. A's CP?
Correct answer: ₹625
Explanation: C pays=A's CP×1.12×1.08=756. A's CP=756/1.2096≈625
