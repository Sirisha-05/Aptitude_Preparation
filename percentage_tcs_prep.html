Formula Cheat Sheet

x% of y = (x/100) × y
x is what % of y = (x/y) × 100
Percentage change = (change/base) × 100
If A is R% more than B, then B is R × 100/(100+R)% less than A
If A is R% less than B, then B is R × 100/(100−R)% more than A
Successive increase a% and b% = a+b+(ab/100)%
Successive decrease a% and b% = a+b−(ab/100)%
n years growth = P × (1 + r/100)^n
n years depreciation = P × (1 − r/100)^n
If price increases by r%, consumption must reduce by r×100/(100+r)%
If price decreases by r%, consumption may increase by r×100/(100−r)%
Fast trick: convert 50%, 25%, 20%, 12.5%, 10%, 5% into fractions instantly.
